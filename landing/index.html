How Divinity Works

Remembers Your Work

Divinity builds a living memory from your emails, meetings, conversations, documents, and projects. It keeps the important context behind your work organized, searchable, and ready whenever you need it.

Creates What You Need

Turn ideas into finished work faster. Divinity helps create emails, presentations, slides, documents, reports, spreadsheets, research summaries, meeting briefs, and other content using the context it already understands.

Takes Action For You

Divinity uses its own browser to work across your apps and the web. It can complete tasks, update information, handle forms, follow workflows, and keep things moving without constant manual effort.

Learns How You Work

Over time, Divinity becomes more personalized to your workflow. It learns your preferences, writing style, projects, and the way you get things done.
